# Shadow Empire

What is Shadow Empire?

![shadow empire header](https://shared.fastly.steamstatic.com/store_item_assets/steam/apps/1154840/00bc94f1d94b3cb21b5faec72bc85d974f8a11f8/header.jpg)

> [Shadow Empire](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1154840/Shadow_Empire/) is a deep turn-based 4X wargame with a unique blend of military focus, procedurally generated content and role-playing features.
> Players take the seat of the supreme ruler of a small nation, with the goal of reconquering a devastated planet.

Shadow Empire is created & developed by [VR Designs](https://www.vrdesigns.net/) and published by [Matrix Games](https://www.matrixgames.com/).
