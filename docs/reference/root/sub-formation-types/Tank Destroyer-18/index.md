# Tank Destroyer

German Panzerkampfwagen I was a German light tank originally conceived as a training vehicle and only equipped with minimal armor and two machine guns as armament. Nevertheless 1,450 Pz I formed a large part of the German armor at the beginning of WW II. Being outmatched on the battlefield by more modern Allied tanks and even other light tanks, the Pz I was supposedly put out of frontline service in 1941 (but apparently not everywhere!). The Pz I was often repurposed as a towing vehicle.
