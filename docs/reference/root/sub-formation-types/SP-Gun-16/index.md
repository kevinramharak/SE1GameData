# SP Gun

The Sturmgeschütz III assault gun was Germany's most-produced armored fighting vehicle of World War II. It was built on the chassis of the Panzer III tank. Initially intended as a mobile, armoured light gun for infantry support, the StuG was continually modified and was widely employed as a tank destroyer. The StuG IIIe has a short-barelled gun, making it effective in battling infantry and fortifications, but not in destroying enemy tanks.
