# n/a

The 88mm Flak was a German heavy anti-aircraft gun designed to effectively fight against modern aircraft. It was a little less powerful than Allied flaks in its anti-aircraft role but it had the advantage of higher mobility. With its high mobility, long range, and high armor-penetration factor, the 88mm Flak could be used with great effect as an anti-tank weapon and was very effective when used in this dual role throughout the war.
