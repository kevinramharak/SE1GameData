# n/a

The 20mm Fliegerabwehrkanone was a light anti-aircraft gun used by the Wehrmacht. It first entered service in 1934 and was used widely throughout the war. It could also be mounted onto half-tracks and tanks to produce mobile anti-aircraft vehicles. Variations of the 20mm Flak were used on German ships and submarines or the four-barreled Flakvierlings.
