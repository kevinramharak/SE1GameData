# Dominatrix

The Dominatrix is an offensive lethal caste of Arachnid. They are sort of as close as an arachnid can get to mechanized warfare. Beware of their onslaught. It is equiped with an organic slugthrower that  launches egg-like super-heated projectiles that can burn through armoured plating.
